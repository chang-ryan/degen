# CRM — cushioned dinosaur re-rating

## One-sentence thesis
CRM is the lower-variance bet that a profitable, washed-out per-seat incumbent re-rates off a floor once the market credits Agentforce as the agentic-pivot it already is.

## Why now
The phoenix winners (SNOW, DDOG, OKTA) have already re-rated this cycle — SNOW +37% in May, DDOG +66% YTD at ~80x fwd, OKTA $90→$135 on 5/28 alone. The crowd is now hunting for "the next OKTA" *among the beaten-down per-seat names it spent the last two years declaring dead*. CRM is the cleanest expression of that hunt: −47% from ATH, but profitable, growing 9%, with Agentforce already at $1B ARR. The rotation is alive; the re-rating window is now, not later.

## The setup
- **What's mispriced:** market is pricing CRM as a per-seat dinosaur in terminal decay. Multiple ≈ 9–10x fwd despite profit, $25B buyback in flight, and a credible agentic product already producing revenue.
- **What the crowd believes:** AI agents collapse per-seat SaaS economics → CRM is structurally impaired.
- **What changes the price:** one earnings print that shows (a) Agentforce ARR accelerating, (b) net-new ARR not collapsing, (c) buyback pace intact. That's enough to short-cover the multiple back to 13–15x — same shape as OKTA's 5/28 move.

## Evidence
- −47% from ATH; ~9% rev growth; profitable; ~$25B buyback authorization in flight.
- Agentforce at ~$1B ARR ramp (mgmt commentary, prior call).
- OKTA template: −60%+ washout SaaS + one clean print on agent-narrative = violent re-rate. Played out 5/28.
- Phoenix peers (SNOW, DDOG, OKTA) already priced — CRM is the un-priced version of the same trade. Asymmetry sits in the names *not yet* re-rated, by construction.

## Risks (real ones, not boilerplate)
- **Earnings disappoints on net-new ARR.** Specifically: if Agentforce ARR misses the implied $1B+ ramp, or if core CRM net-new decelerates further, the "growth re-acceleration" leg of the thesis breaks. *Tell:* the ARR breakouts on the call, not the headline number.
- **Tape-wide de-gross before catalyst.** This is the correlated-book risk: a semis-led de-gross drags SaaS phoenix names with it regardless of the CRM-specific story. *Tell:* SMH down 8–10%+ in a week with no CRM-specific news.
- **Agentforce contribution flat-lines.** Slower take-rate than guided. Slower than the OKTA-style narrative needs. *Tell:* qualitative tone on agent metrics on the August call.

## Invalidation
- Pre-earnings: close below ~$170 on heavy volume (washout into the print, no defenders).
- At/after earnings: net-new ARR decel + Agentforce ARR miss → thesis broken regardless of price reaction; exit the structure even if it's still green on a gap-up.
- Time stop: if 2026-08 print is clean but stock fails to break above ~$220 within 6 weeks, the re-rating crowd-bid isn't there; cut and recycle capital.
